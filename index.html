Marco Pesavento

Biography

PhD Candidate in Computer Vision & Graphics

I am a Ph.D. Student at the Centre for Vision, Speech and Signal Processing,University of Surrey, Uk. My current research is on Super-Resolution Appearance of Human Performance Capture under supervision of Prof. Adrian Hilton and Dr. Marco Volino.

I obtained my M.Sc. and B.Sc degrees from the Information Engineering Department, DEI s, at the University of Padova s, Italy. In 2017, I was awarded with Erasmus+ scholarship s that allowed me to complete the M.Sc. at the Department of Informatic, IFI s of University of Oslo s during which I collaborated with Simula Research Laboratory s for my master dissertation.

Research

I have particular interest in applying computer vision and machine learning to reconstruct, edit and render human performances for application in film, broadcast and gaming. I am investigating novel representations exploiting deep learning to learn animated representations of people for highly realistic rendering. My research objective is to develope methods that reconstruct human performances with high-quality appearance from minimum number of cameras to improve the content production for both traditional media film/games/TV and new forms of immersive augmented and virtual reality experience.
